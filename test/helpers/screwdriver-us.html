Used by Time Lords for over four decades, the Sonic Screwdriver is one of the most iconic and versatile tools this side of planet Jalian 17. Undoubtedly the most masterful handler of this all-powerful tool was the Tenth Doctor; unlocking doors, sinking ships and even triangulating the source of ghoulish ghosts using his trusty device.

Following the incredible success of the Eleventh incarnation and as a result of quite sensational demand, we proudly bring you the Tenth Doctor’s Sonic Screwdriver Universal Remote Control.

Packed with all the amazing functionality as before but this time ingenuously housed inside a smaller unit, this advanced, gesture-based remote can be quickly and easily programmed to control almost all home entertainment equipment. Make iPod docks, TVs and DVD & Blu-ray players bow to your every command and gain complete control with a simple flick, twist or tap of your device. Up to 39 remote control codes can be stored at any one time, perfect for those with an affinity for technology and gadgets.

Don’t go thinking this is just another prop copy in terms of design, oh no, it’s galaxies ahead of the pack in that respect too. Painstakingly 3D scanned from the original screen-used prop, loaned by David Tennant himself, the Tenth Doctor’s Sonic Screwdriver Remote Control is not only one of the most mind-blowingly impressive pieces of technology you can own, it’s also the closest you will ever come to becoming a fully-fledged Time Lord. That’s right, we said loaned by David Tennant himself. Amazing. Featuring genuine extending action, made using the highest-grade materials and sizing up as a complete 1:1 replica, you’ll feel as though you’re holding the real thing.

Officially licensed by the BBC, FX mode plays authentic special effect sounds from the archives of Doctor Who and the bright illuminating tip will light the way no matter which corner of the Universe you find yourself in. Included too is a die cast metal stand complete with Gallifreyan text inscription which also doubles as a magnet making it the perfect, upright display platform for your prized device. As if all of that wasn't enough you can pop your Tenth Doctor’s Sonic Screwdriver Remote Control into its exquisite protective case when not in use, the only way to ensure its safe-passage through time and space.
