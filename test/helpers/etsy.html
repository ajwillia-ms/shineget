    Ms. Mouse is back in our line-up....didn't you miss her?! We think you did as we have had tons of requests to make and new an improved version -- and here she is....complete with ears! So go ahead, click on the unedited photos and zoom in to see the amazing details on this beauty -- then click the add to cart button and snag her before someone else does! We used large white polka dots on red mixed with crisp black details -- like the gathered bodice upper....and of course, we added the feminine detailing of white eyelet. There are tons of super cool finishing touches -- and she's just waiting for you to take her home!

    In addition to our intricate embellishments unique to each design, we are proud to use the best quality cotton on the market -- yep, that's Kona Cotton baby, from Robert Kaufman. Kona has an OEKO-TEX Certification which certifies that no harmful chemicals were used in the production, processing or finishing of the fabric, so not only is it super soft and non-scratchy but it skips the nasty chemical baths in the process.

    *The second to last photo shows an undershirt and a skirt {available in the shop soon} to show you how you can wear this apron as a custom -- for Halloween, parties, or fun! The shirt and pettiskirt are NOT for sale with this listing....they are props only.

    BAMBINOAMORE QUALITY
    - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

    We're a bit OCD about craftsmanship. Each top is fully lined for extra protection and durability -- and there is not one single exposed seam in the entire apron! What?! Yep, everything is enclosed for durability -- so it's not just a pretty face, it's wicked smart too. Even the ties are totally encased. We're proud of our quality and not afraid to show it, just click the product photos above to get up close to our work. Basically this apron ROCKS -- not only as an unexpected, show stopping fashion accessory, but also in the quality of its construction that you can only get at BambinoAmore -- 'cause we don't put our name on crappy stuff!

    Because of this, our aprons are super simple to care for and they are washable in cold on the delicate cycle. Please see our Product Care section in our policies for detailed instructions on how to love your BambinoAmore Apron. https://www.etsy.com/shop/bambinoamore/policy

    ACCESSORIES
    - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

    Our aprons look great with or without an added petticoat. or the under layers of tulle that give the apron skirts added volume. Petticoats are NOT included but can be found in lots of great shops here on Etsy. The petticoats we use in our photos are 15" long.

    - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
    MEASUREMENTS
    - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

    This full-length apron features two short circle skirts, about 13" long, and sized to fit most. From top to bottom, the apron measures approximately 27", it is 12.5" across the top, 30" waist, two 32" ties at the neck, and two 40" ties at the waist -- this means that the ties are long enough to fit a waist size of 26" or more, and can be wrapped to tie in front. Need a bigger or smaller size? Just convo us and we can discuss the options!
